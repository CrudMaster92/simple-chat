Dockside Rules

Each wave you and the rival draw three cards. Choose one to commit; the rival secretly does the same. Highest modified value claims the pennant for that wave.

  • Tideglass: +1 strength from the steady current.
  • Emberwake: Cards 8-12 gather +2 spark.
  • Galeweave: Cards 1-5 gain a tailwind worth +3.
  • Stonebound: On ties, Stonebound claims the wave.

The first to seven pennants wins the match. If the deck runs dry, the leader claims the championship pennant.
