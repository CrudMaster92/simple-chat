    Why EchoTape?

    • Built as a respectful remix of the minimalist Windows Sound Recorder, but with timeline markers, take notes, and lossless exports.
    • Focus boost softly lifts quieter passages while room tone blend keeps ambience consistent across edits.
    • Waveform rendering uses the Web Audio API so you can see energy and pacing as you capture it.
    • Everything runs locally in your browser — perfect for podcast intros, user research quotes, or memory-keeping.
